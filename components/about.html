Farming can be an extremely rewarding activity - when you know how. But many farmers get stuck at (literally) the first step - when they either can't get hold of the seeds they want, or when the seeds that they plant fail to germinate or thrive. We're a team of farmers, who are always looking for something new, interesting and delicious to grow. But one of the most common questions that we're asked when we meet other farmerss is "where do you get your seeds" The AgriShop is here to help with just this - finding seeds for vegetables that are simpler to grow in India and which just taste great. Our goal, is to help you with that first step of your seasonal garden by making high quality vegetable and (some) fruit seeds available to you quickly and conveniently.
